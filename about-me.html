Hello! I'm Guillermo.

I’m a Junior Web developer and aspiring UX designer, based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I love designing and developing websites that prioritize the user to ensure the best possible experiences for every visitor.  Fueled by creativity, I seek to bring ideas to life.

My experience

For two and a half years, I was a Junior Web Developer at Sift, where I worked on the Creative Marketing team.  I did front-end web development, creating pages for and maintaining the Marketing site.  I worked with a wonderful team consisting of another developer and graphic designers to bring the best that the Marketing site could be.

To learn more about my experiences, take a look at my resume!

My journey

Before...

I was a forensic scientist.  I specialized in DNA and biology, but I also did latent print processing and crime scene processing.

After...

My journey into web development began with a Web development bootcamp on Udemy.  I enjoyed it so much I decided to pursue a career in it and earned my certificate in full stack web development from the University of California, Berkeley extension.

Since then...

Since then, I’ve redesigned and helped build Oakland Peace Center’s website and worked as a Junior Web Developer at Sift.  Working with a team of graphic designers at Sift, I gained an interest in UX Design and have been pursuing a professional certificate in UX design from Google.  I aim to one day merge my skills as a web developer with design to build the best possible user experiences.
